Membership Options

Day Pass offers potential members the chance to experience Oxygen for the day including full usage of the locker room facilities.

Weekly Pass allows full access to the facility and classes for seven consecutive days.

Weekend Membership begins Fridays at 5 pm and goes through to close on Sunday and also includes access on holidays.

Student Membership is perfect for full-time high school and college students for one month at a time.

One Month Only Membership gives potential members the chance to fully experience all of Oxygen's offerings with the commitment of just one month.

All Access Membership is a 6-month commitment with a 30-day cancellation policy. If a member cancels and decides to rejoin, they are subject to a rejoiner's fee.

Personal Training options include 30, 45 and 60 minute sessions in different size packages or individual sessions.
